# SoftServe PM corporate training — client brief

_captured: 2026-07-07 · source: client (via Laba engagement)_

## What this is

A corporate training program on **Product Management & product mindset for technical experts**, proposed to **SoftServe** as part of Alex's **Laba** engagement (Laba is the training vehicle; SoftServe is the client).

**Important separation:** the request comes from a SoftServe unit that does **not** know Alex works with their R&D unit. This engagement is **not related** to Alex's current SoftServe R&D advisory / fractional product-lead engagement ([../../../softserve/README.md](../../softserve/README.md)). Keep the two walled off in any client-facing material. The overlap is only that Alex already understands SoftServe's productization challenges and ambitions — useful background, not something to reference in the offer.

## Client context (from brief)

The company is moving **from a service model to a more asset/product-oriented approach**. Services, accelerators, frameworks and reusable assets should be developed **not as one-off per-project solutions, but as products** — with a clear lifecycle, value proposition, roadmap, and adoption strategy.

## Training goal (from brief)

Help technical and delivery leaders:
- shift from engineering/service thinking to **product thinking**;
- understand the lifecycle of products and reusable assets;
- work with value proposition, customer discovery, and adoption;
- build **repeatable offerings** instead of single-project delivery;
- integrate AI/GenAI tools into product management and service evolution;
- develop ownership and an outcome-based approach.

## Target audience (from brief)

~15 participants across **Ukraine, Poland, Bulgaria, US**. Roles: service leaders, capability managers, architects, technical/product owners, delivery leaders, engineering leaders.

## Format expectations (from brief)

Maximally practical, **workshop-driven**. Not only classic PM, but also: platform/productized services; reusable assets; internal products; AI-enabled product operations; service lifecycle management; work on **real company cases** or close-to-real scenarios.

## Topics the client wants included (from brief)

customer discovery · positioning · roadmap thinking · metrics & adoption · product operating model · stakeholder management · monetization / value thinking · product strategy basics.

## Separately of interest (from brief)

- how to transform an engineering capability into a scalable product/service;
- how to work with an asset-based delivery model;
- how AI changes the role of Product Manager and Service Owner.

## What the client expects from us (from brief)

- recommendations on optimal program duration;
- ability to customize to their context;
- profiles of mentors/instructors;
- examples of similar programs or cases;
- recommendations on pre-work / post-training support.

## Key focus (from brief)

Not just theoretical PM understanding, but a **new way of thinking** to transform a service organization into a more product-oriented ecosystem.
