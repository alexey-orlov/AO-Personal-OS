**Total: 22 sessions** = 13 seminars/demos + 9 workshops.

Module topics (verbatim, condensed):
- **M1** — kinds of products/productized assets in a B2B services company (packaged services → technology-enabled services / accelerators → productized services → products → products-as-a-service); product strategy in a service company; productization case studies; productization artifacts & assets; organizational alignment (people, investment allocation, incentives).
- **M2** — human-centered design / start with the customer; lean building; thinking in hypotheses; finding true problems; aligning business goals, product goals, initiatives.
- **M3** — define the idea (market sizing/ICP/segmentation, problem, solution, alternatives & differentiation, business goals & outcomes); discover & validate (customer discovery methods, data analysis); prioritizing product ideas.
- **M4** — product features (co-design/co-dev with customers; prioritizing features & scope; platformization: custom ↔ fixed ↔ dev-configurable ↔ user-configurable); product business model (define model, packaging & pricing, validate).
- **M5** — measuring (feedback loop; activation; retention & engagement; processing qualitative feedback; pathway to enterprise adoption: baby-sitting, feedback sessions, data analysis, evals); planning (prioritization frameworks, roadmapping, stakeholder alignment).
- **M6** — agentic AI to accelerate PM performance (agents, skills, context systems); PM role in the agentic-AI era (blurred PM↔Eng↔Design boundaries); shipping AI solutions to customers (evals & governance).
- **M7** — turning engineering capabilities into repeatable offerings.

## Take-home model (added by me, from PrivatBank pattern)

Each of the 3–4 groups carries **one real reusable asset / service** through the entire program (chosen in M1). Take-home tasks build cumulatively on that one asset across the lifecycle (portfolio position → define → design → measure → AI → next steps), mirroring the PrivatBank single-product-per-group homework arc. Home assignments can run as **homework OR facilitated workshops** (client's choice), same as PrivatBank.
