# Pricing research — SoftServe × Laba PM/product training

_Researched 2026-07-07 (4 parallel market streams: UA market, US enterprise vendors, US facilitator/consultant day rates, services-productization niche). Alex asked to price this from scratch — NOT to anchor on the prior PrivatBank €14,900._

Program priced = **44 live hours (22 sessions × 2h), ~15 senior participants, fully custom, workshop-driven, CPO-level instructor, home-assignment review + follow-up**, sold **through Laba** to **SoftServe**.

FX used: 1 EUR ≈ 1.08 USD ≈ 44 UAH; 1 USD ≈ 41 UAH.

---

## The core finding

**The same program is worth ~5× more in a US frame than a UA frame — and positioning, not content, sets which applies.**

- **UA market** (Laba's home turf, per-program corporate rate): **~€15k mid** (€10k–20k band).
- **US market** (enterprise-vendor + specialist-niche + day-rate buildup, all converging): **~$90–110k mid** ($55k floor → $150k high).

SoftServe is a $1B+ global IT-services firm (US HQ in Austin; delivery in UA/PL/BG/US) — **not** a typical UA training buyer; a $50–90k custom leadership program is routine for them. But it's sold **through Laba** (a UA EdTech whose PM courses retail ~₴30–42k/seat), which **caps the credible ask** unless the deal is reframed. The whole pricing decision is: *how far up the ladder can this be repositioned, and against which budget.*

---

## The three price points (recommendation)

| Tier | Total (EUR) | Total (USD) | Per participant (÷15) | Per session (÷22) | Per live-hr (÷44) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A — UA-commodity (floor; AVOID)** | €15–18k | $16–19k | €1,000–1,200 | €680–820 | €340–410 |
| **B — Premium blended (TARGET / opening ask)** | **€40–55k** | **$43–59k** | **€2,700–3,700** | **€1,800–2,500** | **€910–1,250** |
| **C — US specialist-advisory (stretch)** | €70–110k | $75–120k | €4,700–7,300 | €3,200–5,000 | €1,600–2,500 |

**Recommendation:** open at **~€50k** (framed as tier B), walk-away floor **~€30–35k**. Reach for **tier C ($75–90k+)** only if the discovery call reveals a **US/global budget** + a **transformation (not training) mandate**. **Do not open at tier A** — independent UA market data lands there (~€15k), which _happens_ to equal the prior PrivatBank deal; that convergence is precisely why it must not be the target: it prices a bespoke, senior, advisory program as commodity UA training, which it isn't.

**Why B is the anchor:** at ~$3,000/participant it equals **one** Reforge or Product School seat — for a 44-hour custom program with workshops on their own products + advisory. Unbeatable value story, yet 2–3× above the UA-commodity number, and still a fraction of US vendor pricing so it's hard to reject on value.

## Evidence by frame

### UA market (stream 1)
- **[Fact] Laba PrivatBank comp:** €14,900 / 22 sessions / larger group / homework-checking (internal note). Same seller, same shape — the sturdiest single comp, and the number to _beat_, not match.
- **[Fact] Laba PM retail:** ~₴30–42k/seat ("Product Manager 2.0"; "PM for AI & ML" ₴7k/mo × 6 ≈ ₴42k). 15 seats ≈ ₴450–675k — but corporate-custom is NOT priced per seat.
- **[Fact] Other UA providers (retail):** Projector Product Management ₴116–133k/11mo; "Beginning" ₴11.5k; Genesis Academy free (funnel). Corporate/custom = "on request" everywhere.
- **[Fact] Independent senior UA trainers (per-program method):** A. Sokolov (closest format — online, 2h sessions): **₴12,000/2h session list** (8-pack ₴96k); Andante **₴20,000/training-day** (group of 12–16); Olena Nahirna **€1,000/8h corporate day**.
- **[Fact] Budget fit:** UA tax-exempt training cap ₴25,941/mo/employee (2026) → ₴25,941 × 3mo × 15 ≈ ₴1.17M tax-clean ceiling; a ₴650k program sits well inside it. SoftServe (11k+ staff) has documented $200k/yr-scale L&D budgets.
- **UA band:** LOW €9.9k / **MID €14.9k** / HIGH €19.8k.

### US enterprise vendors (stream 2)
- **[Fact] Boutique day-rate card (Business Training Works):** full day $5,200–8,200; 2-day $9,300–14,700 (off-the-shelf; custom quoted higher).
- **[Fact] Custom multi-session ILT industry band:** **$30k–150k** for a custom program w/ pre-work + facilitation + follow-up (bluecarrot 2026; matches Metis/Learnit $30–150k, senior-exec cohorts $150k+).
- **[Fact] Reforge Enterprise:** ~$60–150k/yr (50+ seats), the only PM-vendor enterprise $ band exposed.
- **[Fact] Per-seat retail anchors:** Product School $2,999–4,999; BrainStation ~$3,150; Product Focus £2,370–2,970; Reforge ~$759–879/seat at scale; Pragmatic $1,195/course. 15 mid-tier seats ≈ $52k with zero customization → the custom floor sits _at/above_ that.
- **All PM-specialist custom pricing = "contact sales"** (Product School for Teams, Pragmatic private, Mind the Product, etc.) — gap.
- **US band:** LOW $55k / **MID $85–110k** / HIGH $150k+.

### US facilitator / consultant day rates (stream 3 — buildup method)
- **[Fact] Individual seasoned facilitator:** $3,000–5,000/day (North Star); **SessionLab 2024 US survey ~$4,000/day** (8h, 15 ppl, in-person) — strongest hard datapoint. Top/veteran expert **$10,000–30,000/day**.
- **[Fact] Fractional CPO (closest to Alex):** $8–16k/mo (Fractionus), $10–20k/mo (PMGuru), $12–25k/mo (Umbrex); ~$196/hr avg; $1,500–4,000/day fractional C-suite. A ~5-month engagement → $50–100k.
- **[Fact] Ex-exec keynote/appearance:** $15–40k professional tier; ex-F500 CEO $25–75k.
- **[Fact] Senior/principal independent consultant:** $3,000–5,500+/day (to $10k), $300–600/hr.
- **[Fact] Development effort (Chapman/ATD):** ILT ~34–43 dev-hrs per finished hr; seasoned expert = own SME, reusing PrivatBank decks → ~20–40:1 or the lighter "1–3 prep-days per delivery-day" facilitation band. Custom ILT dev ~$3,000–6,000 per finished hr → 44h from scratch = $130k+ dev alone (agency/from-scratch view; overstated for Alex, but shows internal-build cost is six figures).
- **Buildup:** ~22 sessions billed as senior half-days (~$3k) ≈ $66k delivery + $20–40k development + review/follow-up ≈ **$90–120k** — independently reproduces the enterprise-vendor mid.

### Services-productization niche (stream 4 — the differentiated anchor)
- **[Fact] Vecteris / Eisha Armstrong** (closest true peer — "Productize"/"Commercialize" author, same niche): consulting engagements **"high-5 to low-6 figures"** ($50–150k+); productized/subscription offerings **"low-5 to high-5 figures"** ($10–90k), priced per licensed seat. A blended training+advisory program sits in the overlap → **$60–150k, center $75–120k**.
- **[Fact] TSIA:** $1,995–2,995 individual; enterprise membership + advisory = contact-sales (est. $25–100k+/yr).
- **[Fact/Inference] Melissa Perri / Produx Labs** (peer transformation advisor): same shape (capability assessment → future state → roadmap) + CPO Accelerator; pricing not public, understood high-5-to-6-figures.
- **[Fact] Commodity PM training** at this hour count sold generically ≈ $20–40k → the specialist/advisory tier is a **~3–5× premium** over it. The premium = productization IP + real transformation advisory, not the seat-hours.
- **Near-unique SKU:** no one sells a 44-hour services-productization _program_ off the shelf → Alex is a price-maker, not a price-taker.

## Levers that move SoftServe up the ladder (probe in discovery)
1. **Which entity / budget pays?** SoftServe US/global L&D → US frame credible (tier B–C). UA cost center → capped near UA rates (tier A–B). *Biggest single factor.*
2. **Training budget vs. transformation/consulting budget?** 5–10× difference. The program is genuinely advisory (drives productization decisions) → can legitimately be sold against the consulting line = tier C.
3. **Is the ~€15k Laba-course comparison already in their head?** If so, break the "PM course" frame early — reposition as bespoke leadership + transformation program.

## Caveats before quoting
- **Laba sets the client-facing price and takes a cut; Alex's take-home is a split.** This research is ammunition to push Laba's B2B team to quote at tier B/C rather than defaulting to UA-course pricing. Align with Laba on **both the number and the split** before quoting.
- **Do not quote per-seat** to SoftServe — invites "why not just buy 15 Laba seats at ₴42k?" Quote a **program fee**; show per-participant only as value justification (how Sokolov/Andante and the PrivatBank deal were structured).
- **The offer's `[€ — to be confirmed]` placeholder** stays until Alex+Laba decide; recommended fill = tier B (~€50k) as the opening ask.
