## Shape

- **7 modules / 22 sessions** (Alex's strict backbone вЂ” no modules/topics added), **2h each**, **online/live** (audience spans UA/PL/BG/US).
- ~**15 participants**: service leaders, capability managers, architects, technical/product owners, delivery & engineering leaders.
- **3вЂ“4 groups**, each carrying one real reusable asset/service through the whole lifecycle via cumulative home assignments (**~2вЂ“3h each**; can also run as facilitated live sessions, client's choice per module).
- Three session types: **interactive seminar** (theory + instructor's examples + discussion on their cases), **workshop** (collaborative group exercise в†’ real artifact + a decision), **home assignment** (group work between sessions).
- **M6 practical work = AI-powered PM** (agents/skills/context systems accelerating the participants' own PM workflow вЂ” focus areas + first experiments); evals & governance remain seminar/demo content only.
- Final module = **defining next steps** (transformation roadmap + owned commitments).
