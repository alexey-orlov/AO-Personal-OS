## Snapshot

- **Program structure v1 (authoritative — Alex-authored 2026-07-14, [Google Doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1wJ9Pn5RiPDn_gGmtf9tDy5zv5SNxOT5lERbewpNuPOg/edit); chat, 2026-07-14):** core concept = a shared AI "work OS" (governed repo of product context + reusable skills/sub-agents, MCP integrations to live systems, access rules/guardrails), built and proven on the customer's real artifacts via a working-group model — champions (own customer-side groundwork) + power adopters (commit to live-work application), up to 5-10 people, all trainings open to the whole product team.
