# Reading shortlist

Books Alex has flagged to read or listen to later. Maintained by the `book-shortlist` skill
(`/book-shortlist`) — send a title (even shortened or misspelled) and it's resolved, categorized,
and appended below. The `book-finder` skill can find/acquire anything listed here.

**Categories** (the same areas of interest `book-finder` routes on — keep in sync with
`.claude/skills/book-finder/references/sources.md` §1):

- **Fiction** — philosophical novels, historical novels with philosophical meaning
- **History** — countries, events, biographies, memoirs
- **Non-fiction (psychology / business / technology)**
- **Non-fiction (philosophy)** — popular/general, not academic
- **Other** — fits none of the above

**Entry format** (one per line; append-only, newest at the bottom of its section):

`- **Title** — Author[, year] · added YYYY-MM-DD[ · was: "raw input"][ · note]`

The optional `was:` records the original text when it was corrected from a misspelled/shortened input.
