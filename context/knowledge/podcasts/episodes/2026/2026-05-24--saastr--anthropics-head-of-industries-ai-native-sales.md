## Summary
Elenore Dorfman describes how Anthropic rapidly rebuilt its go-to-market motion after Opus 4.6 drove a surge in demand by making Claude the connective tissue across existing tools. Rather than hire aggressively, the team threaded Claude into Salesforce, Slack, Intercom (Finn), Clay, Ironclad, Gong and others to automate qualification, proposals, forecasting, ticketing, and bespoke asset creation. The result: a hybrid self-serve + sales funnel, encoded best-practices as AI-driven 'stills', and a system that shifts managers to review-and-coach work.
