## Summary
Dan Shipper argues that AI will change how we work in two linked ways: company-level agents for delegation and desktop coding-agent surfaces (Codex/Claude Co-work) as the new work operating systems. Rather than eliminating jobs, automation will create more human work — reviewing, gardening, and operating agents — and new high-value roles (PMs, full-stack designers, forward-deployed engineers) will thrive if they "ride the models."
