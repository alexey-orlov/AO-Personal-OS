## Summary
The conversation argues that AI is redirecting enormous amounts of economic value into a small set of model and platform companies, and that the structure of token economics, supply constraints, and who controls models will decide who captures that value. Venture firms and founders must adapt: outcomes are larger and faster, winners are highly concentrated, and early-stage playbooks and support must change accordingly.
