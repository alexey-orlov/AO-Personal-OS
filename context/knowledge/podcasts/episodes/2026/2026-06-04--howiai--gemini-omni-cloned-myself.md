## Summary
The host uses Google's Gemini Omni (via the Flow creative suite) to create an AI avatar of herself and generate a one-minute hype video for her podcast in about 15 minutes. The episode argues that multimodal video models democratize end-to-end video production—storyboarding, generation, and editing—while still producing inconsistent, sometimes uncanny results that require care to make believable.
