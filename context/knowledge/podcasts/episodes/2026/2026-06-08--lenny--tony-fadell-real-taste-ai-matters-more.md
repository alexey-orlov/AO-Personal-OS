## Summary
Tony Fadell explains how great products are made by small teams empowered to make opinion-driven decisions, iterating through multiple generations while designing the entire system (product, installation, marketing, distribution). He warns that AI lowers the barrier to building but increases the premium on disciplined product taste, architecture, and storytelling — otherwise AI-driven work produces brittle, short-lived results.
