## Summary
A keynote arguing that the SaaS downturn is ending because AI agents are creating enormous, immediate ROI and a Cambrian explosion of new B2B apps. The central claim: companies that build or embrace agentic products and agent-friendly APIs will dramatically accelerate, while legacy, non-agentic products will wither. Practical advice: train agents intensively, buy good agent platforms when possible, and make your API agent-friendly.
