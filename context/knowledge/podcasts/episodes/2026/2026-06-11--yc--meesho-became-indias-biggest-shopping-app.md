## Summary
Vidit Aatrey, co‑founder of Meesho, traces the company's evolution from local-shop experiments to WhatsApp‑based resellers and finally a mass consumer shopping app. The throughline is relentless, customer‑first observation and rapid product pivots — kill what doesn't work, double down when you find power users — and apply the same discipline now to AI and voice to reach the next billion users.
