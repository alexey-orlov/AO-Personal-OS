## Summary
The founder of Groww explains how early product pivots, relentless customer obsession, and design focus created product-market fit and organic growth. He argues that full transparency, playing only in regulated markets, and prioritizing customer love over early monetization reduced risk and unlocked durable monetization later. He also describes practical founder practices — being a power user, direct customer outreach, and written shared values — that kept four co-founders aligned while scaling.
