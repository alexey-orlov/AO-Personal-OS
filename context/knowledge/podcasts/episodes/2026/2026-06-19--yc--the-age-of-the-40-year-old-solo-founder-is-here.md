## Summary
Bryant Chou explains Ploy, an AI-first platform that ingests existing sites and company data to auto-generate modern, on-brand websites and continuously run marketing and product optimizations. The throughline is that curated model stacks plus productized integrations let experienced founders (and even single operators) execute growth, design, and engineering work orders that used to require teams. Bryant argues this creates a new era where taste and domain experience, amplified by AI, matter more than raw labor.
