## Summary
This talk gives a practical, stage-based playbook for landing your first 10 customers, arguing that early wins come from founder-led, manual work rather than tools or automation. The central throughline: customers 1–3 come from personal networks, customers 4–10 require unscalable, high-touch tactics (in-person meetings, small events, community outreach), and only after ~10–20 customers do prospecting tools and scaling make sense.
