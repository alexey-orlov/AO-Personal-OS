## Summary
PayPal and Salesforce discuss how they deployed AI agents (Agent Force) in production to reengage thousands of previously ignored leads, raise meeting conversion rates, and free sellers to focus on high-value conversations. The throughline is pragmatic: you don't need perfect data to start, headless integrations (Slack/API) let nontechnical users work with CRM data, and successful scale requires cross-functional governance and ongoing agent curation.
