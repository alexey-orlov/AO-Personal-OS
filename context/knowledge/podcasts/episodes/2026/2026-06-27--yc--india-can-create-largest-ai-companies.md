## Summary
Speakers argue that India is uniquely positioned to build the next generation of global AI companies because of deep technical talent, cheaper talent density, and a global, non-local AI revolution. The talk emphasizes practical founder behaviors — tinkering, shipping, iterating, and using compute liberally — and explains why younger founders and second-mover, product-focused teams can win today. It also covers tactical advice (apply to YC, use credits, experiment with tokens and open models) and the equity challenge of access to high-end compute.
