## Summary
Andrew Ambrosino explains how frontier AI is reversing traditional product processes: implementation is cheap and ubiquitous, so the new scarcities are taste, curation, and timing. He argues teams must pick the right medium (document vs prototype), preserve domain specialties while enabling role fluidity, and build artifacts now that can be re-evaluated as model capability improves.
