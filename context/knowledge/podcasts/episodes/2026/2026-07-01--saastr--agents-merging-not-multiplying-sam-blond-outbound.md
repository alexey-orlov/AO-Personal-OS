## Summary
The episode shows how integrating an AI agent across existing SaaS (Salesforce, Bill.com, QuickBooks, Brex, Panda) turns it from a narrow helper into a single, operational VP of finance that automates contracts, invoices, reminders and even commission calculations. The hosts argue you should start with a real, high‑pain use case (collections) and that properly built agents make outbound work again while changing how GTM teams allocate human effort.
