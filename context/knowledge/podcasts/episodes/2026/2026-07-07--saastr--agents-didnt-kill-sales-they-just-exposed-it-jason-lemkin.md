## Summary
Jason Lemkin walks a room through how generative agents and AI are changing B2B SaaS: they don't simply replace salespeople but reveal broken processes, accelerate product cycles, and force new hiring and GTM practices. He gives concrete advice on planning cadence, where to hire GTM talent, which sales tasks agents already outperform humans at, and how to judge AI 'slop' versus value. He also sketches implications for engineering hiring and what VCs care about today.
