## Summary
An interview with a writer (Greg) about how he integrates large language models into his creative and technical work without surrendering his voice. He argues LLMs are best used as research assistants, infrastructure accelerants, and low-cost builders for one-person software, while deep writing requires strict attention boundaries and deliberate craft. The conversation balances practical examples, cost numbers, and philosophical takes about agency and anthropomorphism.
