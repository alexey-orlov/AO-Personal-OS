## Summary
Adam Mosseri explains how AI is reshaping product teams, workflows, and content on Instagram: it accelerates execution, pushes organizations toward smaller cross‑functional pods and generalist "product staff," and makes taste and human authenticity comparatively more valuable. He argues that AI will change what people do (planning, reviewing, curating, steering models) more than it will eliminate high‑level judgment and strategy, and that new model capabilities (embeddings + LLMs) let platforms make recommendations more legible and give users agency over their feeds.
