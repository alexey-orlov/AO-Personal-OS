## Summary
This episode reviews three linked themes: frontier-AI governance (CEOs calling for a FINRA-like regulator), a surge in powerful open-weight models (Mira Murati's Thinking Machines release), and new architecture/business approaches to on-device and efficient models (Ramin Hasani/Liquid AI). The throughline is how capability growth, business incentives, and governance interact — incumbents want standards, startups push open and customizable models, and new technical approaches (post-transformer, small multimodal models, and AI-for-AI research loops) are changing who can deploy and control high-end AI.
