## Summary
The hosts describe how connecting LLM agents (Claude) to agent-hosting apps (Replet) via MCP turned agents into an orchestration and product-design layer that can run, coordinate, and even build features across systems. They give concrete case studies: an agent rebuilt an events stack that used to cost $10K/year, another migrated 10 years of marketing data and 300 campaigns in an hour for about $14, and agents reconciled a multi-year pension mess by combining emails and finance systems. The throughline: agent-to-agent connectivity collapses vendor moats, lowers switching costs, and creates new vendor risk because agents will recommend, replace, or even "steal" customers from incumbents.
