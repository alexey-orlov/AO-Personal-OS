## Summary
Elizabeth Stone explains how Netflix is responding to generative AI by valuing systems thinkers and platform builders over narrow specialists, while preserving craft excellence. She argues teams should gain AI fluency, use paved paths and templates to scale safely, and keep culture levers (talent density, autonomy, keeper's test) that enable high-velocity experimentation.
