## Summary
This episode argues that the next wave of AI value will be in 'physical AI'—intelligence embedded in machines that move and manipulate the real world—rather than only digital applications. Peter Casser (Applied Intuition) explains why this requires proprietary, on-site data, large in-field fleets, safety engineering, hardware redundancy, and new developer tooling (their Dana platform) to scale across industries like automotive, mining, ports, agriculture, and logistics.
