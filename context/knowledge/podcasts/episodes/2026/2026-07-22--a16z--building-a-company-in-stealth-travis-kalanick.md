## Summary
Travis Kalanick recounts why he built his new venture (Adams / CloudKitchens / City Storage Systems) in stealth after Uber and lays out a unified framework for digitizing the physical world. His central argument is that industries can be treated like computers—manufacturing as CPU, real estate as storage, and transport as network—and that starting with a focused beachhead (food) lets you build the infrastructure, talent and management capacity to scale into mining, transport and other industrial AI opportunities.
