## Summary
James Vicar of New demonstrates how agentic RevOps—AI-guided selling built into a unified CPQ and billing platform on Salesforce—speeds quote-to-cash and reduces rework. The throughline: encode pricing, product, and approval guardrails into an AI-driven playbook so reps generate valid quotes, contracts, and invoices without handoffs to finance. The result is faster closes, fewer invoice corrections, and clearer auditability for finance.
