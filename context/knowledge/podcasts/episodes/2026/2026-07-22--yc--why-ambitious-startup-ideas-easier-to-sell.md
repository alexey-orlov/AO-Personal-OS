## Summary
A founder (James of PostHog) argues that being bold makes hiring, fundraising, and marketing easier, and explains how AI is changing product development. The conversation ties together practical lessons from multiple pivots, how to operationalize model-driven engineering, and why founders should simultaneously hold an audacious long-term vision and ruthless short-term shipping discipline.
