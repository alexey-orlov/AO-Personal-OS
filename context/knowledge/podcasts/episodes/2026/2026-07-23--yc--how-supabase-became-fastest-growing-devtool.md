## Summary
Paul, the founder of Supabase, explains how product choices (Postgres + fully open source), obsessive developer experience, and emergent AI agent distribution allowed Supabase to scale from a small side project to a major devtools platform. He traces the early product and go-to-market decisions, the metrics they optimized (time-to-value), how agents changed distribution and product needs, and why the company is now betting on self-driving databases as its next moat.
