## Summary
This interview explains why the founders of Dust built a horizontal, model-agnostic AI platform aimed at changing how people work, rather than training their own models. The central argument: intelligence will become commoditized and labs will not be the only source of value, so product-layer companies should remain model-agnostic, adopt usage-based pricing, and pursue defensibility through network effects and collaboration features.
