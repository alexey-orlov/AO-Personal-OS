## The throughline
Capability is no longer the constraint for shipping AI products; reliability is. The scarce engineering work is the unglamorous last mile — error recovery, guardrails, determinism — that decides whether an agent ships at all. _(synthesis, not evidence)_

## Insights

### Agent reliability, not capability, is the 2026 bottleneck
John argues the model can already do the task; the product problem is making it do the task the same way a thousand times. He estimates 80% of his team's effort is error recovery, guardrails, and determinism — the unglamorous last mile that decides whether an agent ships.
